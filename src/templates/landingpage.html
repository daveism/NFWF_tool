Welcome to Coastal Resilience Assessment and Siting Tool (CREST)!

This web tool is used to explore Resilience Hubs along the coastlines of the contiguous United States. Resilience Hubs are large areas of natural, open space that can be used to make informed decisions about the potential of restoration, conservation, or resilience projects to achieve dual benefits by protecting human communities from storm impacts while also providing important habitat for fish and wildlife.

CREST also includes eight in-depth assessments of targeted watersheds that incorporate more specific fish and wildlife datasets as identified by local stakeholders.
